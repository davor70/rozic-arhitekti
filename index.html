About the Studio

Design through
collaboration

Studio Rožić Architects and Partners works on the design and realisation of a broad scope of housing, industrial and commercial projects. Our architects and engineers collaborate intensively — yet in a relaxed manner — believing that both the built and natural environment offer ample opportunities for improvement and development through design.

Founded in 2007 by Igor and Marija Rožić in Rijeka, Croatia, the studio has grown steadily over the years. In 2012, Maja Čukelj was promoted to partner. Today, the core team consists of six architects, expanded on a per-project basis from a trusted pool of collaborators.

Our most recent project is the master planning and architectural design for the JGL Pharma Valley development in Rijeka, Croatia.

Our Approach

Where does
inspiration begin?

We look for inspiration everywhere — in nature, technology, history and social interactions. Our dedication to each project begins with a focus on functionality and efficiency within budget constraints, then extends towards exploring design options that deliver the optimal impact of the new building or space.

Our responsibilities towards the client are considered with the utmost attention, and by performing our craft in the public realm, we hold ourselves accountable to the community as well.

Technology

Built on
BIM

Through each new project we strive for improved coordination, design control and change management — freeing up time and energy for the project team to focus on what they do best: design.

Clients and project managers gain access to in-depth project analysis and efficient tracking via the same database we use to develop all our projects — the Building Information Modelling (BIM) system Autodesk Revit, supported by Revit Server infrastructure that allows geographically distributed team members to work simultaneously on a single project file in real time.
