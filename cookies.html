Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2024  ·  Rožić Architects & Partners d.o.o., Rijeka, Croatia

This website uses cookies and similar technologies. This Policy explains what they are, which ones we use, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixel tags and local storage, which function in a comparable way. This Policy covers all such technologies collectively referred to as "cookies".

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential operation — to ensure the website functions correctly, including remembering your cookie preferences.
  • Analytics — to understand how visitors interact with our website, which pages are most visited, and where visitors come from, so we can improve the user experience.
  • Preferences — to remember settings you have chosen, such as language or display preferences.

We do not use cookies for advertising, behavioural tracking or to build profiles for third-party marketing purposes.

4. Essential vs. Non-Essential Cookies

Essential cookies

These are strictly necessary for the website to function. They do not require your consent under the ePrivacy Directive and Croatian law (ZZPO). You cannot opt out of essential cookies while using the website.

Non-essential cookies (analytics & preferences)

These require your prior consent. When you first visit our website, a cookie banner will ask for your consent. You may accept all, accept only essential cookies, or customise your preferences at any time.

5. Third-Party Cookies

If we use Google Analytics, Google LLC may set cookies on your device. Google processes this data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy. We have enabled IP anonymisation so that your full IP address is never stored by Google.

We do not embed third-party social media plugins, advertising networks or external video players that would set additional third-party cookies without your knowledge.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

Via our cookie banner

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent notice. You can choose to accept all cookies, essential cookies only, or customise your selection. You may change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

Via your browser settings

Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. The methods vary by browser; please refer to your browser's help documentation:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites you visit.

7. Legal Basis

Essential cookies are set on the basis of our legitimate interest in operating a functional website (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR). Non-essential cookies are set only with your prior consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), in accordance with the ePrivacy Directive and the Croatian Electronic Communications Act (Zakon o elektroničkim komunikacijama).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation or our practices. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
